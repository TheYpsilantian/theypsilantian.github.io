Yousef Rabhi

Washtenaw County Commissioner, District 8 · Former Michigan State Representative · 2026 Ypsilanti Mayoral Candidate
Who He Is

Yousef Rabhi represents District 8 on the Washtenaw County Board of Commissioners — returning to the county seat he previously held before serving in the Michigan Legislature as State Representative. He is documented in The Ypsilantian's archive as a 2026 Ypsilanti mayoral candidate, making him one of the most significant emerging actors in the accountability stories The Ypsilantian covers. His entry into the mayoral race — as a county commissioner with legislative experience — represents a different profile than either Brown (the incumbent) or Fellows (the council challenger).
