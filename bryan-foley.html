Bryan Foley

Vice President, Supreme Felons Inc. · YDDA Board · CVI Committee, Washtenaw Sheriff · Ward 1 City Council Candidate, August 4, 2026
Who He Is

Bryan Foley is a convicted federal bank robber (BOP #39861-039), confirmed by A2Indy. He serves as Vice President of Supreme Felons Inc., which received $1.2 million in Washtenaw County ARPA funds. He sits on the Ypsilanti Downtown Development Authority (YDDA) board. He serves on the Community Violence Intervention (CVI) Committee at the Washtenaw County Sheriff's Office. He is running for Ward 1 City Council of Ypsilanti in the August 4, 2026 primary, challenging incumbent Me'Chelle King.
